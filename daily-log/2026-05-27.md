### System Improvement Suggestion

> **Add JSON-LD structured data to `index.html` for Google rich results.** Right now Google indexes your pages as plain text — no additional signals about what LeftClick is, who founded it, or what services you offer. Adding a `<script type="application/ld+json">` block to `index.html` with `Organization` schema (name, url, logo, description, founders, sameAs LinkedIn/social links) takes ~15 minutes and gives Google machine-readable facts about your business. This directly improves how your brand appears in branded search results — you can get a knowledge panel, rich sitelinks, and founder attribution faster. Unlike a sitemap (which helps discovery), structured data helps *presentation* in search. Paste this into the `<head>` of `index.html`:
>
> ```json
> {
>   "@context": "https://schema.org",
>   "@type": "Organization",
>   "name": "LeftClick",
>   "url": "https://leftclick-agency.netlify.app",
>   "description": "AI automation systems for B2B agencies, SaaS, and professional services.",
>   "founders": [
>     { "@type": "Person", "name": "Nick Saraev" },
>     { "@type": "Person", "name": "Noah Edis" }
>   ]
> }
> ```
>
> Wrap it in `<script type="application/ld+json">` tags. Then validate at [schema.org/SchemaVersion](https://validator.schema.org/). Submit to Google Search Console. Done.
