We had a client spending 2 hours researching each prospect before a single email went out.

Company size, tech stack, recent news, buying signals. Manual, every time. 30 prospects a week = 60 hours of prep work.

We built a lead research system: scrapes LinkedIn + company sites, pulls recent triggers (funding, hiring, product launches), writes a personalized first line, drops everything into an outreach queue.

Result: 2 hours → 12 minutes per prospect. 30 prospects researched and queued in a morning instead of a week.

We build systems like this for agencies and SaaS teams at leftclick-agency.netlify.app
