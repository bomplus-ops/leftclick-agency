## Draft

We sent 4,200 cold emails for a client over 6 weeks.

They had tried cold email before. Low open rates. Zero replies. Gave up after two campaigns.

We rebuilt the system from scratch:
→ ICP tightened to a single niche (agency owners, 10–50 employees, US-based)
→ 3 personalization variables pulled from LinkedIn on each lead
→ Sequence: Day 1 intro, Day 4 case study, Day 8 short close
→ Sending from a warmed secondary domain, not their main one

Results after 6 weeks: 38% open rate. 4.1% reply rate. 11 qualified calls booked.

Their previous campaigns averaged sub-1% reply rates. The difference wasn't volume — it was targeting and personalization.

If your cold outreach isn't converting, the problem is almost never the copy.
