### System Improvement Suggestion

> **Add a `sitemap.xml` to accelerate Google indexing.** Right now Google has to crawl and discover all 4 pages organically. A sitemap tells Google exactly what exists and when it was last updated — this speeds up indexing, especially for a new site. Create a file called `sitemap.xml` in the repo root with 4 entries (index, services, about, contact), each with a `<lastmod>` date and `<changefreq>weekly</changefreq>`. Then go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/) → your site → Sitemaps → Submit URL: `https://leftclick-agency.netlify.app/sitemap.xml`. This takes 10 minutes and directly improves how fast the site ranks for branded searches. Without it, Google may take weeks to discover all pages.
